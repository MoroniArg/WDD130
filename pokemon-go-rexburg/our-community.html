Our community

Our Pokémon GO community in Rexburg is thriving! We have an active group of trainers who regularly attend small, medium, and large events. Whether you're a casual player or a hardcore raider, there's always an opportunity to connect, battle, and explore together. With fantastic local parks and gathering spots, we ensure a fun and engaging experience for all.

Join our Pokémon GO Rexburg Discord to connect with local trainers, coordinate raids, share tips, and have fun—everyone is welcome!
